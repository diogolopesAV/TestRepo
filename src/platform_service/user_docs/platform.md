Welcome to the Riverty AI Platform — your central hub for discovering and governing AI capabilities across the organisation.

## Getting Started

The Platform Home is your entry point into the AI estate. From here you can navigate to any active module using the sidebar on the left.

### What you will find here

- **Recent activity** — a snapshot of the latest skill updates, readiness signals, and platform announcements.
- **Quick links** — shortcuts to the most commonly used modules for your role.
- **Guided tour** — if you are new, click *Start guided tour* in the sidebar to get a hands-on walkthrough of the platform.

### First steps

1. Check your assigned role in the account section at the bottom of the sidebar. Your role determines which features are visible to you.
2. Browse the **Skills Registry** to discover what AI capabilities are already available.
3. Run an **AI Readiness** assessment for your team to understand where to focus adoption efforts.

## Navigation

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. Each item corresponds to a platform module.

### Modules

| Module | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Overview, news, and quick access |
| **Skills** | Browse, create, and manage AI skills |
| **AI Readiness** | Assess and track team AI maturity |

### Collapsed sidebar

Click the chevron button at the top-right of the sidebar to collapse it to icon-only mode. Click it again to expand.

### Docs Mode

You are currently reading docs. Toggle the **Docs** button in the topbar at any time to switch between the live platform and its documentation. Navigation stays active — switching pages while in Docs Mode shows the docs for that page.

## Roles & Access

The platform adapts to three personas. Your visible features and default views depend on your assigned role.

### Business user

Focused on consuming AI capabilities. You can browse the Skills Registry, view readiness reports, and submit feature requests.

### Developer

Can browse, create, and update skills via the Skill Builder. Has access to the SKILL.md editor and GitHub pull-request workflow.

### Administrator

Full access including platform configuration, user management, and governance controls. Admin features are gated behind the admin role.

### Requesting access

If your role assignment seems incorrect, submit a feature request via the **Feature requests** button in the sidebar. The platform team will review and adjust your access.
